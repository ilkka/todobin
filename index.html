TodoBin by ilkka

A Qt Quick todo list app using Remember The Milk as a backend.

Dependencies

Qt 4.7.x (probably 4.7.2+)

Install

Load up the project in Qt Creator and build .debs for your Maemo device or .rpms for your MeeGo device.
